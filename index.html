nicole r burch

ux designer

i am a motivated and organized ux specialist, who developed my creative thinking and problem solving during my years of experience working in coordination and customer service.

it all started when...

i was born in anaheim, ca and was fortunate enough to grow up going to disneyland weekly. this let me explore my creativity starting at a young age. once i received my design degree, i moved to austin to mix that ever-growing creativity with my love for technology. after then spending a year in italy, i relocated back to california.

with a background in events and customer service, i have mastered my creative thinking and problem solving skills. i love utilizing both of those, so being in a creative field is where i thrive.

outside of work, you’ll find me volunteering at the local animal shelter, running outside, exploring with my 2 100lb dogs, or seeing live music around town.
